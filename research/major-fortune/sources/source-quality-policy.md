# Source Quality Policy

This policy governs the acceptance of Zi Wei Dou Shu sources into the Major Fortune Calculation Core.

## Principles
1. **Mathematical Determinism over Popularity:** Popular online interpretations do not establish correct structural logic. Every calculation must be traced to a formalized mechanical rule.
2. **Provenance is Mandatory:** A rule without an attributed source is an `unverified` claim and cannot enter the Calculation Core.
3. **School Segregation:** Contradictory rules from different schools (e.g., Nam Phái vs Trung Châu) must be recorded separately and isolated via the School-Policy Matrix.
4. **Heuristics are Not Structure:** Named patterns (cách cục) or generalized sayings (phú) are heuristics for the Semantic Layer, not calculation overrides for the Core.

## Quality Tiers

### 1. Primary (`primary`)
- **Definition:** Foundational texts (e.g., Tử Vi Đẩu Số Toàn Thư, Tử Vi Đẩu Số Tân Biên, Vương Đình Chi's core publications).
- **Usage:** Can be used to define structural relationships, generation rules, and data structures.
- **Conflict Handling:** Conflicts between primary sources must be managed via explicit School Policies.

### 2. Secondary (`secondary`)
- **Definition:** Widely accepted commentaries, modern textbooks, or academic analyses.
- **Usage:** Used for corroboration and disambiguation. Cannot override a primary source unless a specific School Policy elevates it.

### 3. Reference Only (`reference_only`)
- **Definition:** Prose interpretations, forum debates, or minor school offshoots.
- **Usage:** May support semantic notes in the Semantic Layer. **Prohibited** from defining calculation rules.

### 4. Unverified (`unverified`)
- **Definition:** AI-hallucinated rules, un-sourced blogs, or contradictory claims lacking textual backing.
- **Usage:** **Prohibited** from both Calculation Core and Semantic Layer.

## Claim Resolution
- Claims are split into atomic structures. Do not store entire chapters.
- If a source claims an exact life event will occur (e.g., "Mệnh Vũ Khúc Đại Vận Kình Dương chắc chắn tai nạn năm 32 tuổi"), the deterministic event prediction must be stripped. Only the structural combination (Vũ Khúc + Kình Dương in Đại Vận) is stored.
