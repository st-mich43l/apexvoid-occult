# Limitations — Huyền Khí Ontology V0.1

- **No final formula exists.** V0.1 defines terminology, symbolic dimensions,
  source/claim governance, a rule contract, school and conflict policy, and an
  expert fixture workflow — nothing more.
- **No numeric score, no evaluator, no analyzer, no UI.** No ordinal→number
  mapping. Magnitude words are ordinal vocabulary, not coefficients.
- **Classical statements may conflict**, and the two schools differ; the
  ontology surfaces contradictions instead of silently resolving them.
- **External Tử Vi Cổ Học outputs are not ground truth** — benchmark/held-out
  comparison data only, never a runtime input or doctrinal authority.
- **No production use is authorized.** Production modules must not import this
  research package.
- **Expert review is required** before any evaluator work. PR #95 may not begin
  until at least 30 fixtures are approved.
