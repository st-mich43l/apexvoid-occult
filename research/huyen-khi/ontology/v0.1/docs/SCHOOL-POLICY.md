# School Policy

Two research schools are isolated: **Nam Phái** and **Trung Châu**. `shared`
knowledge is knowledge both schools accept.

## Isolation rules

- Nam Phái cannot use Trung Châu-only rules, and vice versa.
- `shared` must be explicit — never inferred from whichever fields exist.
- A missing/unresolved school profile is **invalid knowledge** (fails closed).
- No cross-school fallback. `ruleFallbackToOtherSchool = false` for both.

Shared physical fact namespaces (both schools inherit): physical-palace,
canonical-star, brightness, void-marker, natal-transformation,
relation-geometry, five-element.
